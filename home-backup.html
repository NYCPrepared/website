Community

We train local community leaders and relief professionals how to use our cutting edge tools and help them communicate their needs to our software developers who expand functionality and improve usability. The result is rapid capacity-building for participating nonprofit organizations and rapid innovation for connected software projects.

The Open Source Advantage

All of our software tools are free, libre, and open-source. That means our local community — not commercial third party software vendors — has complete complete control over our software and data. That results in our systems having better security, more flexibility and much lower operating costs than proprietary solutions. Best of all, our systems benefit from contribution from software developers around the world — and they benefit from our improvements too.

Services

Organization in NYC involved in coordinated relief and resilience work can receive free websites and access to our world-class information management solutions. They can also receive subsidized services from our trusted technology partners.
